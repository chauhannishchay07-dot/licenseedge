Frequently Asked Questions

How much can we typically save?

▼
Most clients save between 20-40% on their annual Adobe spend. The amount depends on your current plan, usage patterns, and team size. This is why we do the free audit first — to give you an exact number for your situation.

What if we don't save any money?

▼
You pay nothing. That's our promise. We only charge 20% of the savings you receive. Zero savings = zero fee. We're confident in our process, which is why we're comfortable with results-only pricing.

How long does the audit take?

▼
The initial audit call is about 20 minutes. You'll share your current Adobe setup and spending. We'll send you a detailed analysis within 48 hours. If you want to proceed, the negotiation typically takes 2-4 weeks.

Do we need to switch Adobe plans?

▼
Not necessarily. Sometimes the savings come from better pricing on your current plan. Other times, we recommend a different mix of licenses that gives you better value. We'll show you all options and let you decide.

Will this affect our current service?

▼
No. We time the transition to happen during your renewal. You won't experience any downtime or service interruption. If we recommend changes, we'll make sure your team is ready before switching.

What about support and service levels?

▼
You get the same Adobe support and service levels. We're negotiating pricing and license optimization, not changing your support tier. You still have access to all Adobe resources.

How do you make money? How do we pay you?

▼
We charge 20% of the first year's savings. So if we save you $10,000, you pay us $2,000. That's it. No hourly fees, no retainers, no hidden costs. Your savings pays our fee.

Can you work with our reseller?

▼
Yes! We work with resellers and direct Adobe accounts. In fact, we can sometimes work with your reseller to get even better terms. Tell us your current setup during the audit.

Do you serve international companies?

▼
Yes. We help businesses in the US, UK, Australia, and New Zealand. Adobe pricing and terms vary by region, and we have expertise in all these markets. If you're elsewhere, reach out and we'll see how we can help.

What data do you need from us?

▼
Current Adobe plan, number of licenses, annual spend, team size, and usage patterns. We can pull some of this from your Adobe account. You don't need to prepare anything — just be ready to discuss how your team uses Adobe.
