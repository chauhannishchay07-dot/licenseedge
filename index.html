Our Story

Born From the Inside

LicenseEdge started with a simple observation: businesses are paying 30–50% more than they need to for Adobe — and nobody is helping them fix it.

Large consulting firms only care about enterprise deals. Adobe's own sales team has no incentive to reduce your bill. Small businesses don't have procurement teams to fight back.

We stepped in to fill that gap. With deep knowledge of Adobe's licensing structure, pricing levers, and negotiation windows, we give businesses the same advantage Fortune 500 companies have — without the enterprise price tag.

Today we serve creative agencies, marketing firms, and businesses across the US, Australia, and New Zealand — helping them reclaim thousands every year.
